about the founder

The name Abeja (Spanish for Bee) Media was inspired by the simplistic beauty of one of nature's most integral parts: the bee. Bees have a symbiotic relationship with their environment and provide nourishment to their surrounding environments. Similarly, websites and content creation are integral to business. Abeja Media aims to circumvent traditional media services by providing professional services in web development & design, photography, and content administration & creation. We believe in providing an experience that is both customer-centric and of the highest quality.

Ashley Bauler founded Abeja Media in 2020; however, has extensive experience in design, photography, technical writing. She began photographing in the 7th grade with a Canon point and shoot camera. Ashley became enamored with the power that photography has to convey a story and emotion at a specific point in time. She went on to become a staff photographer and writer for the University of Colorado newspaper, "The Sentry". Ashley has photographed numerous concerts at Red Rocks, The Ogden, The Gothic, and other venues that are popular in the Denver music scene. Ashley has covered professional sporting events and interviewed an array of professional athletes. During her time at the University of Colorado newspaper, she gained skillsets in Adobe Creative Cloud products, i.e. Lightroom, Photoshop, InDesign, Dreamweaver.

While Ashley was studying finance in her undergraduate she taught herself how to program and code at her internship who assigned her to build and maintain the firms website. This experience sparked her interest to learn more about web development and design. She studied web development at the University of Denver and managed websites for charity organizations before starting Abeja.
